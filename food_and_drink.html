FOOD & DRINK


The Farm Table is all about taking food off the farm & into the fire, celebrating the simplicity of beautiful ingredients grown, reared, caught & crafted on our doorstep. We aim to get as much from our farm onto your table as possible and if it’s not from our farm it’s from our vibrant community of like-minded artisan food producers, farmers & fisherman who share our passion. Our menu changes regularly to reflect the seasons, using what is bountiful in the fields. On Sundays, you can enjoy our roast cooked over fire alongside our regular menu.

  • The Passage House Inn
  • Ferry Road, Topsham, Exeter, Devon, EX3 0JN
  • Tel: 01392 873653
  • -
  • About

  • Tucked away in the quaint estuary town of Topsham, the 18th-century Passage House Inn boasts one of the most picturesque waterside views in East Devon. Gaze across the tranquil River Exe as you linger over an al fresco lunch, see the boats sail in over a sunset pint of real ale or watch local wildlife such as avocets and swans as you enjoy a morning coffee. With a combination of traditional features and a bright modern interior, the relaxed ambiance of this historic inn makes it a popular choice with residents and visitors alike, whether you choose to dine in the popular garden area, the cosy bar, or welcoming restaurant.

  • About

  • The Galley is a charming seafood restaurant in historical Topsham, overlooking the River Exe estuary. From these surrounding waters comes the freshest, much sought-after fish and seafood. With such a bountiful larder of Devon’s coast and countryside, the family-run team proudly serves the very best of Devon on a plate.
